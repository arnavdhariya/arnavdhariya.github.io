Bio

I am an undergraduate Computer Science student at UC Irvine specializing in intelligent systems, with research experience across privacy-preserving machine learning, computational biology, distributed systems, and climate-focused literature analysis. My work is centered on building rigorous experimental pipelines and algorithmic tools that help answer difficult real-world questions with measurable evidence. I enjoy research that sits between theory and systems implementation, especially when it requires careful benchmarking, simulation, and reproducibility. Outside the lab, I write technical essays, play harmonium, and spend too much time refining plots and abstractions.
